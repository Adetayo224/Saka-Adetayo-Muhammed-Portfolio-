About Me

Robotics Researcher & Engineering Student

I am currently completing my Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering student at Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH), specializing in robotics and autonomous systems. My research focuses on developing safe, intelligent robotic solutions that can interact seamlessly with humans and environments.

As the lead of the Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Innovator Network (RAIN-IN) and President of SPE LAUTECH Chapter, I'm committed to advancing robotics education and research in Nigeria and beyond. I'm currently seeking opportunities for graduate studies in Robotics and Autonomous Systems.

My work spans from embedded AI and real-time systems to digital twins and simulation, always with an emphasis on safety, reliability, and practical applications in agriculture, manufacturing, and service robotics. I've won multiple hackathons and competitions, demonstrating my ability to translate theoretical knowledge into practical solutions.
